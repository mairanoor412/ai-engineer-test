CASE STUDY: Coca-Cola "Share a Coke" Digital Revival Campaign

Client: The Coca-Cola Company
Agency: Ogilvy Global
Campaign Duration: May 2024 - August 2024
Budget: $35 Million

OBJECTIVE:
Revive the beloved "Share a Coke" campaign for the digital age, driving personalization at scale through AI-generated content and social sharing. The goal was to increase summer sales by 10% and grow the brand's social media following by 500K across platforms.

TARGET AUDIENCE:
Primary: Young adults (18-30) who are active on social media
Secondary: Families with children (parents aged 30-45)
Psychographics: Social, experience-driven, values nostalgia and personal connection

STRATEGY:
Coca-Cola partnered with an AI platform to allow consumers to generate personalized digital Coke labels with their names, custom messages, and AI-generated artwork. Users could share these digital labels on social media or order physical personalized bottles through an e-commerce integration.

Key tactical elements:
1. AI Label Generator: Web app where users create custom Coke labels with AI art
2. Social Sharing: One-click sharing to Instagram, TikTok, and WhatsApp
3. E-commerce Integration: Order personalized physical bottles directly
4. Vending Machine Experience: Smart vending machines in malls with instant personalization
5. Partnership: Collaborated with Spotify for "Share a Song + Coke" playlist feature

CHANNELS:
- Social Media (Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp, Snapchat)
- Web App (shareacoke.coca-cola.com)
- E-commerce (direct-to-consumer personalized bottles)
- Retail (smart vending machines in 200+ locations)
- Streaming (Spotify integration)
- Influencer Marketing (50 macro-influencers + 500 micro-influencers)

CREATIVE APPROACH:
Tone: Fun, personal, nostalgic yet modern, inclusive
Visual Style: Bright colors, bold typography, AI-generated artistic flourishes
Music: Upbeat summer playlist curated with Spotify
Tagline: "Share a Coke, Share a Moment"

RESULTS:
- 8.5 million personalized labels generated in 3 months
- Summer sales increased 12% (exceeding 10% target)
- Social media following grew by 750K (50% above target)
- Web app had 15 million unique visitors
- E-commerce: 500,000 personalized bottles ordered
- TikTok: #ShareACoke2024 reached 3.2 billion views
- Smart vending machine interactions: 2 million+
- Campaign ROI: 4.2x return on investment

KEY LEARNINGS:
1. AI personalization dramatically increased engagement vs. static name labels
2. WhatsApp sharing was surprisingly effective in markets outside the US
3. Smart vending machines had highest conversion rates in shopping malls
4. Spotify integration drove 25% of web app traffic through playlist sharing
5. User-generated content from the AI label generator was shared 3x more than brand content
6. Personalized physical bottles had a 40% re-order rate

BUDGET BREAKDOWN:
- Technology/AI Platform Development: 30% ($10.5M)
- Media Buying: 25% ($8.75M)
- Influencer Marketing: 20% ($7M)
- Content Production: 15% ($5.25M)
- Smart Vending Machines: 10% ($3.5M)
